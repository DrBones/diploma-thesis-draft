Since only specific elements of the retarded and lesser \gfnc{} are required one can fortunately circumvent the costly full inversion in equation \ref{eqn:finitegreensfunction} and make use of specialized algorithms to reduce computaion time. The implementation of the recursive \gfnc{} algorithm presented in chapter \ref{sec:rgapresentation} will here be described in further detail.
